How it works

Fans are crying out to have a say in the teams they love

See the way it works, how you can influence your team and get rewarded! FC SHERIFF FAN TOKEN solves this.

Sign up

Download the app & create an account in seconds. We just ask for your phone number & email

Buy tokens

Fan Tokens can be purchased or hunted for free in our in-app game from time to time

Vote

Make your decision on team matters. With binding polls, the team has an obligation to bring your choice to life

Participate & win

Take part in polls & in-app games, rack up reward points and unlock VIP experiences, team merch & much more!
